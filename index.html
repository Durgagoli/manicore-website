    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are a few questions we get regularly from our users.

    Why do educational institutes need an LMS? ▾
    An LMS centralizes all educational content, tracks student progress, enables remote learning, and automates administrative tasks — saving time and improving outcomes for everyone in the institution.
    What are the key features of Manicore EDU's LMS? ▾
    Key features include live and recorded classes, automated attendance, OMR scan analysis with 14 insights, a 300K+ question bank, adaptive learning algorithms, finance management, and a fully customizable student portal accessible on mobile and web.
    What is an ERP system in educational institutes? ▾
    An Educational ERP integrates all operations — admissions, finance, attendance, academics, and communication — into a single unified platform, eliminating data silos and giving administrators complete visibility and control.
    What are the common modules of Manicore ERP? ▾
    Manicore ERP includes 12+ modules: Data Management, Finance Management, Admission Management, Attendance Tracking, Live Classes, Question Bank, Performance Analytics, Communication Hub, Biometric Integration, Online & Offline Exams, Customizable Student Site, and Virtual Classrooms.
    How can institutes ensure successful implementation? ▾
    Our team provides complete onboarding, staff training, data migration support, and dedicated customer success managers with 24/7 technical support throughout the implementation journey.
