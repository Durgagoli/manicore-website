Manicore EDU Cancellation Policy

We understand that sometimes plans change. Our cancellation policy ensures a smooth and fair process for all users.

  • 1. Refunds: Once paid, it will not be refunded.
  • 2. Cancellations: No cancellations are allowed.
  • 3. Contact: For any queries, contact our support team at support@manicoreedu.com.
  • 4. Subscription Plans: You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings.
  • 5. Advance Notice: Cancellations made within 7 days of payment may be eligible for a full or partial refund (see Refund Policy).
  • 6. Third-Party Fees: Manicore EDU is not responsible for cancellation fees applied by third-party services.
  • 7. Cancellation Process: Submit a request via dashboard or email support.
  • 8. Confirmation: You will receive confirmation once cancellation is processed.
  • 9. Access Post-Cancellation: Access continues until the billing cycle ends.
  • 10. Data Retention: Data will be stored for 1 year unless deletion is requested.
  • 11. Reactivation: Accounts can be reactivated within 30 days.
  • 12. Non-Refundable Charges: Setup and training fees are non-refundable.
  • 13. Custom Agreements: Custom contracts may have separate terms.
  • 14. Partial Cancellations: Specific modules can be canceled with adjusted pricing.
  • 15. Outstanding Dues: All dues must be cleared before cancellation.
  • 16. Policy Changes: We may update this policy anytime.
  • 17. Feedback: We welcome feedback to improve our services.
