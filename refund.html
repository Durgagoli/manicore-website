Manicore EDU Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us. This refund policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are provided.

  • 1. 7-Day Refund Window: If you are not satisfied with our service, you may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
  • 2. After 7 Days: Refund requests after 7 days will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
  • 3. Request Process: Email support@manicoreedu.com with your registered email and order details.
  • 4. Refund Timeframe: Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days.
  • 5. Eligibility: Only payments made directly to Manicore EDU are eligible for refunds.
  • 6. Partial Refunds: Partial refunds may be issued depending on usage.
  • 7. Non-Refundable Items: Setup fees, training charges, and custom work are non-refundable.
  • 8. Duplicate Payments: Duplicate transactions will be fully refunded after verification.
  • 9. Chargebacks: Chargebacks without contacting support may result in account suspension.
  • 10. Refund Denial: Refunds may be denied for misuse, fraud, or policy violations.
  • 11. Service Disruption: Refunds are not provided for scheduled maintenance or temporary outages.
  • 12. Account Termination: Terminated accounts are not eligible for refunds.
  • 13. Refund Communication: All refund updates will be shared via email.
  • 14. Currency: Refunds will be processed in the original payment currency.
  • 15. Policy Updates: We may update this policy anytime with prior notice.
  • 16. Contact: For queries, contact support@manicoreedu.com.

We aim to be fair and transparent with every user.
